About Us

Few Words About Us

We are a group of dedicated commuunity working towards educating people in tech, removing myths about tech education, spreading awareness about opportunities in tier two & tier three cities and introducing tech content that is helpful for everyone.

STARTED IN 2017

GirlScript aims to become world's biggest tech community for learning technical skills and getting opportunities around the globe. We support diversity in tech and thus we ensure 60% women participation in every meet ups, workshops and conferences.

OUR GOALS

To empower people and specially under represented groups by education them on technology and related necessary skills. To create local opportunity, growth and impact via tech education in every country around the world and to bring diversity in tech domain.

IMPACT CREATED

Our initiatives have affected 30,000+ students which include 56 cities in India, 4 International Chapter. We ensure 50% female participation
